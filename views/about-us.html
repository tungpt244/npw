About us

Our Expert Smash Repair Team

  • Button

At Northern Panel Works (NPW), all panel work and spray painting is performed on-site at our centrally located workshop - located just off the Stuart Highway in Pinelands. Our workshop offers plenty of off-street parking, amenities for people with disabilities, an air-conditioned office and multiple phones for processing fast claims.

With just over 350sqm of production space, we are capable of working on 20 cars per week on-site. However, we can increase this number to hundreds of cars, in the case of widespread hail storm damage. We have a network of trusted contractors to help cater for those big events.

At Northern Panel Works (NPW), our aim is to ensure each vehicle receives personalised attention to ensure it leaves our workshop meeting manufacturers' specifications while also looking factory-showroom finished.
