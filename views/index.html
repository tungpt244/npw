Our Panel Beaters Can't Be Beat For Quality Workmanship

Located in Pinelands, Northern Panel Works (NPW) is the preferred smash repairer for all private, insurance and fleet vehicle work throughout the Top End, including Darwin and Palmerston.

If you've had an accident (minor or major) we'll be there to ensure your vehicle is repaired to factory standards - affordably and quickly. With NPW, you'll get transfers to and from our Pinelands workshop, a hire car as well as your vehicle cleaned with any repair work.

You'll be in safe hands at Northern Panel Works. Our expert panelbeaters know what's involved in repairing your car, 4WD, truck or light commercial and will be more than happy to provide you with a quote while you wait in our air-conditioned lounge area.
