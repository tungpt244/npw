About Us

Located in Pinelands, Northern Panel Works (NPW) offers smash repairs and panel beating for private, insurance and fleet vehicles throughout the NT, including Palmerston and Darwin. Our well-equipped workshop allows us to provide spray painting, heavy and light accident repairs, plastic welding, body kits, resprays and more for all makes and models of vehicles.
